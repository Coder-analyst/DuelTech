Our Story

As students of SY AIDS B at K. J. Somaiya Institute of Technology (KJSEIT), we often found ourselves and our peers struggling to make informed decisions when purchasing tech products. The market is flooded with options, making it increasingly difficult to compare and choose the right product that fits both needs and budget.

This challenge inspired us to create DuelTech - a platform that simplifies tech product comparisons. Our goal is to help students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts make better purchasing decisions by providing detailed comparisons, specifications, and user reviews all in one place.

What sets us apart is our focus on providing clear, unbiased comparisons and our commitment to keeping our information up-to-date. We understand the importance of making informed decisions, especially when investing in technology.
